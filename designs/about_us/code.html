About Us

Preserving the essence of human stories for a resilient future.

We are a global non-profit dedicated to documenting the intangible cultural heritage of communities at risk, ensuring that the wisdom of the past informs the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Story

Founded in 2012, onewholefuture began as a small collective of anthropologists and filmmakers concerned by the rapid disappearance of oral traditions in the digital age.

What started as a single field project in the high Andes has evolved into a global network of "Memory Guardians"—local archivists trained to capture and preserve their own community's narratives through high-fidelity media.

Our Role

We act as a bridge between ancestral knowledge and modern research. By digitizing endangered languages, craftsmanship techniques, and ecological wisdom, we provide open-access resources for scientists, educators, and the communities themselves.

Our role is not just to collect, but to empower. We invest in local infrastructures, providing the tools and training necessary for people to reclaim and celebrate their own histories on their own terms.
